About Coastal Oak

Napa Valley's Premier Remodeling Experts

Located in the heart of downtown Napa, California, Coastal Oak Construction takes pride in our strong connections with many of the top wine manufacturers in the region. Our team is passionate about enhancing the beauty and functionality of homes in this vibrant community.

With a commitment to quality and craftsmanship, we strive to create spaces that reflect the unique lifestyle and culture of Napa Valley. Our attention to detail and dedication to customer satisfaction have made us the preferred choice for discerning homeowners throughout the region.
